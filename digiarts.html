Digital Arts




Arts is not just about traditional art. In our generation today, everything can be digitalized through technologies and one of them is digital arts. Join us now and be a digital artists!




What is Digital Arts?


Art created with software, computers, or other electronic devices is referred to as digital art, sometimes known as computer art or new media art. Digital art includes animations, pictures, graphics, films, digital paintings, and other works created or generated using digital media.





What is the purpose of Digital Arts?


Digital art has a wide range of applications and uses, although it is most typically utilized in business. These involve developing visual graphic effects and animations in films, video games, and other media. Because it is so simple to create, share, and sell digital art, numerous new vocations and careers have opened up for digital artists all around the world. Graphic designs may also be created with digital art.
