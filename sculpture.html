What is Sculpture?


Sculptures are one of the most well recognized kinds of art and one of the earliest forms of visual art. Sculpture is a popular form of both visual and fine art. It takes the shape of three-dimensional things made of hard or plastic materials. Traditional sculptures are reliefs or freestanding pieces. Contemporary artists, on the other hand, may utilize sculpture as part of "experimental art," in which sculptures are integrated into surroundings or tableaux that might immerse the viewer.





What is the purpose of Sculpture?


People have used sculptures in the past to express their religious ideas, which is one of the most prevalent uses. The church had no way of communicating the message of hope and the calamity that would befall those who disobeyed the word long before humans learned to read and write. Sculpture, being one of the earliest forms of art, predates the invention of painting and hence served as the sole means of communicating the importance of religion.
