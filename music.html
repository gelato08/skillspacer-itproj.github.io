What is Musical Instruments?


Musical instruments are electronic devices that generate musical sounds. Instruments also reflect physical geographies and express individual and communal identities. A musical instrument's function in society can be diverse: it can be used for amusement or to play a vital part in ritual; its use in an ensemble can represent social cohesion; and owning a certain instrument can elevate an individual's status.





What is the purpose of Musical Instruments?


The use of musical instruments to better understand social lives, local and global economies, key political events, physical geographies, religious belief systems, and other aspects of human activities in which music plays a role has grown as interest in the disciplines of ethnomusicology and musicology has grown.
