What is the purpose of Photography?


Photography may be used for a variety of reasons and has several elements. Photography has the ability to convey a narrative, record a moment in time, chronicle, and be art. Photography has various technical as well as social and creative applications. Essentially, photography's aim is to convey and capture historical events.
